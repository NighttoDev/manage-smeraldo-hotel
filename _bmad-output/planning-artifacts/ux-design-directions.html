Housekeeping Mobile Experience

The housekeeping view is a completely separate mental model from reception. Staff need speed and simplicity on their phone, not a scaled-down version of the desktop app.

Task-only view — only the rooms assigned to this staff member are visible. No financial data, no other staff records.
One-tap updates — "Mark Ready" is the primary action. One tap. No confirmation dialog needed.
Progress bar — visual progress toward today's task list builds a sense of accomplishment.
Priority flagging — reception can flag rooms that need urgent attention. Housekeeping sees it immediately.
Bottom nav — 3 tabs max. My Rooms, Done Today, Profile. Nothing else visible.
